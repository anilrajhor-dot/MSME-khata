A suite of free, offline-capable financial tools for MSME credit appraisal, accounting, and bank loan preparation. All data stays on your device — nothing is uploaded anywhere.

📊 CMA Data Generator — Bank Credit Appraisal

🏭

CMA Generator — Existing Unit

For units with 1–2 years of actual data. Enter Trial Balance or directly fill P&L / Balance Sheet. Auto-generates all 5 CMA Forms, Ratio Analysis, DSCR, MPBF, Drawing Power, IRR/NPV, Break-Even, and EMI Schedule. Supports Trading, Manufacturing, and Service industries.

CMA I–V DSCR MPBF IRR / NPV Break-Even EMI Schedule Excel Import/Export Trading / Mfg / Service
🆕

CMA Wizard — New Unit

For new businesses with no historical data. Enter just 5 inputs — Capital, Fixed Assets, Estimated Sales, Cost Ratios, and Holding Norms — and get a complete 5-year CMA instantly. Ideal for first-time borrowers and greenfield project appraisals.

5-Step Wizard Auto Projections Project Cost MPBF DSCR IRR / NPV Break-Even EMI Schedule

About these tools: All apps run entirely in your browser — no installation, no server, no internet required (except for Excel import/export). Data is stored locally on your device. Use the Export/Backup option in each app to save your data to Google Drive regularly.

For bank submissions: CMA Generator outputs are prepared as per RBI/Tandon Committee guidelines and are suitable for submission to Central Bank of India and other public/private sector banks.
